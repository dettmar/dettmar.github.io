Open vs closed mode

Closed mode

  • Your default state at work
  • Slightly anxious
  • Active, exciting
  • Not much humor
  • Purposeful
  • But; Not creative!

Open mode

  • Allows for creativity
  • Relaxed
  • Less purposeful
  • Humoristic
  • Curiosity for it
  • Playfulness
  • No pressure, play!

Example #1

the invention of penicillin

  • Cultivating bacteria cultures on dishes
  • The next day, all but one had grown bacterias
  • Instead of discarding it as a mistake
  • He became curios of why it hadn't appear
  • Boom; the penicillin!

Example #2

the discovery of graphene

But, we need both!

  • Open for pondering a problem
  • Once we have a solution, switch to closed mode
  • Otherwise we cannot get things done
  • Keep alternating open and closed mode throughout the process
  • Reality check; we too often get stuck in closed mode
  • So much magic happens in the production

Preparation

  • Create an "oasis" of space and time
    • Set a start and end time (usually 1.5h)
    • No other todo's for now
    • Trivial things that are urgent are easier to do than important things that are not urgent
  • 30 min to get into the open mode
  • Don't stress the solution, original ideas don't necessarily come quick/easy
  • Tolerate the discomfort of not having a solution
  • Give yourselves enough time, and the solution might pop up in the shower later
  • Confidence, fear of making a mistake - absolute no no!
  • Allow each other to be stupid/illogical/funny
  • Create an environment for trust and enjoyment for all
